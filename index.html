About me

Hi, I am a Full Stack Developer and Networking Specialist who has been interested in programming for 3 years. In my work as a Full Stack Developer, I strive to create web applications in accordance with the latest standards and trends. I am involved in designing and implementing both the frontend and backend of applications to ensure the best possible user experience.

My projects

I have worked on various projects over the years, including a calculator in C#, a SIP server that is connected to VoIP through VPN, and a number guessing game. I have also developed a to-do list app, which has proven to be very useful in my personal and professional life. I am constantly seeking out new challenges and opportunities to further develop my skills as a Full Stack Developer and Networking Specialist.

Why full stack?

I have always been fascinated by technology and the limitless possibilities it presents. As I began to explore the world of programming, I quickly discovered that being a Full Stack Developer would allow me to work with a variety of technologies and platforms, while also providing a deeper understanding of how applications are built from the ground up. With my interest in both frontend and backend development, I found that becoming a Full Stack Developer was the perfect fit for me. I enjoy being able to see the entire process of application development, from concept to deployment, and have found that the Full Stack approach allows for greater efficiency and collaboration throughout the development process. Additionally, I appreciate the opportunity to work on diverse projects and to constantly learn and adapt as new technologies emerge. Becoming a Full Stack Developer has allowed me to pursue my passion for programming in a meaningful and fulfilling way, and I look forward to continuing to develop my skills in this exciting field.
